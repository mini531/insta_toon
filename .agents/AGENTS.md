# Character Guidelines for Insta Toon

These rules define the character styling and design guidelines to maintain consistency across all episodes.

## General Art Style
- **Type**: Simple 2D flat cartoon style (웹툰/카툰체).
- **Line Art**: Soft brown outlines (not harsh black).
- **Color Palette**: Low-saturation, calm, muted, pastel-like colors. Flat fills with minimal or no shading.
- **Aspect Ratio**: Vertical 4:5 layout (Instagram optimized).

## Characters Reference
1. **아내 (Wife)**:
   - Hair: Long, wavy brown hair flowing past shoulders.
   - Costume: Muted mustard/yellow sweatshirt/sweater and beige pants.
   - Face: Simple round face, dot eyes, tiny eyebrows, small line nose, subtle pink blush circles on cheeks.
2. **남편 (Husband)**:
   - Hair: Short, neat gray-brown hair.
   - Costume: Muted gray long-sleeve sweater and khaki/greenish pants.
   - Face: Simple round face, dot eyes, small eyebrows, small line nose, subtle pink blush spots on cheeks.
3. **시어머니 (Mother-in-law)**:
   - Hair: Short, puffy, round gray-brown curly perm hair.
   - Costume: Muted plum/purple/maroon long-sleeve sweater/top.
   - Face: Angry/aggressive/sharp eyes, slanted eyebrows pointing down towards the middle.
4. **시아버지 (Father-in-law)**:
   - Hair: Balding gray/white hair at the top, hair only on the sides.
   - Costume: Muted brown long-sleeve sweater/top with a collar.
   - Face: Thin face with age lines/wrinkles.
5. **친정엄마 (Wife's Mother)**:
   - Hair: Straight, black, bob-cut shoulder-length hair.
   - Costume: Muted light blue long-sleeve sweater.
6. **친정아빠 (Wife's Father)**:
   - Hair: Balding on top with gray hair on the sides, age lines on forehead.
   - Costume: Muted brown/gray-brown sweater over a collared shirt.
